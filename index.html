What's in This Program?
6 core modules — fully personalised to your lung capacity
MODULE 01
📋
Assessment & Baseline Testing
  • SpO₂ & Peak Flow testing
  • 6-Minute Walk Test
  • Breathlessness scoring
  • Personalised treatment plan
⏱ 45 – 60 mins
MODULE 02
💨
Breathing Re-Training
  • Diaphragmatic breathing
  • Pursed-lip breathing
  • Pranayama & breath control
  • Relaxation breathing techniques
⏱ 30 – 45 mins / session
MODULE 03
🫁
Airway Clearance Therapy
  • ACBT protocol
  • Postural drainage
  • Percussion & vibration
  • Huffing & forced expiration
⏱ 20 – 30 mins / session
MODULE 04
🏃
Exercise Conditioning
  • Low-impact aerobic training
  • Strength & endurance exercises
  • SpO₂-monitored progression
  • Energy conservation strategies
⏱ 30 – 40 mins / session
MODULE 05
📚
Patient Education
  • Understanding your condition
  • Inhaler technique & medication
  • Trigger avoidance & lifestyle tips
  • Home exercise program
⏱ 20 – 30 mins / session
MODULE 06
📈
Progress Review & Discharge
  • Repeat functional testing
  • Baseline vs. current comparison
  • Long-term home plan design
  • Follow-up schedule planning
⏱ 45 mins
